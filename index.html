Cattle Breeder Game

Goal: You are a cattle farmer who owns the herd below. Breed your cattle to get as many heavily-muscled cattle as possible.

Directions: Select pairs of cattle to breed (reproduce). Each female can produce up to two offspring in a generation. At the end of the generation, select 6 offspring to breed (reproduce) in the next generation. Choose at least 4 females and 1–2 males. Continue breeding in each generation until you have met your goal.
